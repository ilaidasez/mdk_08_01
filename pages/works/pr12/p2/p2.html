DANIELLE

History,and Big Hit Global Audition Passing

Danielle was born Danielle Marsh/Mo Ji-hye on April 11, 2005 in Newcastle, New South Wales, Australia. Her father is an Australian man named Andrew Marsh, and her mother is a South Korean native named Mo Ji-won (모지원). Danielle has one older sister, Olivia Marsh/Mo Gyu-na (모규나), who was born in 2000.

She was a student at Munsan Elementary School. When she was three years old, Danielle moved with her family to South Korea, where they remained until 2012. In October 2019, Danielle returned to South Korea after passing BigHit's Australian Global Audition and became a trainee.

2010-2012: Child model and television personality Danielle began working as a child model and South Korean TV personality

in 2010 under the name Mo Jihye, appearing in numerous TV shows and advertisements. An official Fancafe and Twitter account remain from this period. Her television career ended upon moving back to Australia. 2022: Debut with NewJeans On July 21, 2022, NewJeans released a pre-debut music video featuring Danielle for their song "Attention". They officially debuted on August 1, 2022 with the EP New Jeans.2023: Debut with voice acting On May 2, 2023, Danielle was teased as the voice of Ariel in the Korean dub of the upcoming 2023 The Little Mermaid movie. On May 17, Disney Korea released an official performance video for “Part of Your World”, featuring Danielle alongside clips of the upcoming movie.
