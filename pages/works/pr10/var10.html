GET UP

от 07.07-COMEBACK

Get Up is the second EP by NewJeans. It was released on July 21, 2023 with "Super Shy", "ETA" and "Cool With You" serving as the EP's title tracks. Two pre-release tracks, "New Jeans" and "Super Shy" were released digitally on July 7, 2023.

HOW SWEET

от 27.04-COMEBACK

How Sweet is the second single album by NewJeans. The single album was released via CD, Vinyl LP, and digitally, on May 24, 2024 at approximately 1PM KST with "How Sweet" serving as the album's title track. A pre-release track, "Bubble Gum" was released on April 27, 2024.The M/V for “How sweet” was released on the same day, around 3 hours KST after the single album was released.
